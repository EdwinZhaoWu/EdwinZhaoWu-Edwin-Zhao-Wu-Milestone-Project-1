Bonobos Share A Total of 98.7 Percent Of Human Genetic Makeup!

The correct answer to the first question is 98.7 percent! Our closest cousins, bonobos share 98.7 percent of our genetic makeup. They are about two-thirds the size of humans and are covered with dark hair. They are distinguished by their black faces, red lips, two or three webbed toes, a tail tuft, and parted long hair.
